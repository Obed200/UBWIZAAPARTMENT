To provide exceptional accommodation experiences by offering comfortable, affordable, and well-equipped apartment that feel like home. We strive to make every guest's stay memorable through personalized service and attention to detail.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for hospitality, UBWIZA Apartment emerged from the need for quality, affordable accommodation in the growing Kanombe area. Our name "UBWIZA" means "beauty" in Kinyarwanda, reflecting our commitment to creating beautiful experiences for our guests.

We understand that traveling can be stressful, which is why we've created spaces that offer the comfort and convenience of home combined with the professionalism of hotel services.
